What Is It?

The CCI Peer Mentors are hosting a Code Golf event for first-year CCI students to show off their coding skills, grab free food, and win prizes! During the event, participants will be asked to discuss their solutions to the puzzle and talk about any challenges or lessons they found along the way.

We recommend that you have a complete solution by the event.
